Charles Anthony Paris (born April 30, 1980), known professionally as Brother OTG, is an American record producer, composer, and music executive. His production work includes collaborations with artists such as Beanie Sigel, Tone Trump, The Real DJ Damage, The Heatmakerz, and many more.[1] His work has also been featured on major television networks including BET, MTV, and VH1. As a voting member of the Grammy Awards' Philadelphia and Chicago Chapters for over a decade, he has also contributed significantly to the music industry behind the scenes.[2]

Paris is also an educator in the producer community, creating tutorials and masterclasses for a variety of digital audio workstations (DAWs) and hardware, including the Akai MPC series and Roland SP-404 MKII.[3]

Early life and career

Paris was born in Urbana, Illinois. He developed an early passion for music, creating and selling mixtapes and later selling music for the local "Rap House." In the late 1990s, he moved to Decatur, Georgia, where he worked at the 285 Flea Market and began installing car stereos.[2] At age 19, he attended Atterbury Job Corps before formally beginning his career in music under the name "I Double L".

While building his music brand under his first label, Midwest Ill Muzik Group (ASCAP), he also trained and worked as a Journeyman Maintenance Mechanic. During his 30s, he relocated to Philadelphia, establishing himself as one of the notable producers in Philadelphia. There, he founded Over Time Grind Productions, LLC, where he serves as CEO and Founder, and focused on the music business, managing artists and building brands. Concurrently, he had a successful career in automotive sales, selling over 2,000 cars in the tri-state area between 2015 and 2020.[1]

Currently, Paris focuses on teaching music production and serves as an advisor and consultant for brand building and music business strategy through his brand, SupplyTheWork.

Services

Music Licensing: For content creators, filmmakers, and businesses in need of high-quality, original music, Paris provides a straightforward licensing service. He offers a diverse catalog of production-ready tracks and can create custom compositions to perfectly match the tone and style of a project, ensuring all music is cleared for use on platforms like YouTube, podcasts, and commercial advertisements.

Brand Consulting: Leveraging his years of experience in both the music and business worlds, Paris offers strategic consulting for artists and creative entrepreneurs. This service is designed for those looking to build a sustainable brand, develop effective marketing strategies, and navigate the complexities of the music industry. He provides actionable advice on everything from social media presence to revenue stream diversification.

Producer Mentorship: Aimed at aspiring and intermediate producers, this one-on-one mentorship program offers personalized guidance to accelerate skill development. Sessions are tailored to the individual's goals, covering topics such as advanced production techniques, workflow optimization, sound design, and the business side of being a professional producer.
